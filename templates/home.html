Problem Statment
Problem Statement: Fresh fruits and vegetables are perishable, and improper freshness management leads to waste. Retailers and consumers lack tools to accurately assess freshness and estimate shelf life. There is a need for a solution that uses computer vision and machine learning to analyze visual cues (color, texture) and predict freshness, helping reduce food waste and optimize inventory.
Introduction
Food waste from spoiled produce is a major issue in retail and homes. This project uses computer vision and CNNs to assess the freshness of fruits and vegetables and predict their shelf life by analyzing visual cues, helping reduce waste and optimize inventory management.
Proposed Solution
Freshness Detection and Shelf Life Prediction for Fresh Produce This solution uses CNN to analyze images of fruits and vegetables, assessing their freshness and predicting their shelf life based on visual cues like color and texture. It helps reduce food waste by enabling retailers and consumers to make better decisions about inventory and consumption.
